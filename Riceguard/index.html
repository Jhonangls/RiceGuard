Installation Instructions

For Android:

  1. Tap the “Android” button.
  2. If redirected to Google Drive, tap the Download button.
  3. Once downloaded, open the APK file.
  4. Allow installation from unknown sources if prompted.
  5. Tap Install.
  6. Open the app when done.

For iOS:

  1. Tap the "iOS" button.
  2. Once redirected, tap the share icon (bottom center).
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen".
  4. The app will appear on your Home Screen.
